About me

Hi, and nice to meet you!

I am Larissa, a Brazilian that have moved to Stockholm, Sweden in 2019. Back in Brazil I was studying architecture and in the middle of the process fell in love with Signage, what means sign language. I decided to follow my passion and now I am here studying Digital Media in a Hyper Island graduating course. My motivation is make life joyful and easier with good design.
