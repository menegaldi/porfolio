Goalplan

January - February / 2021

What is Goalplan?

GoalPlan is a Swedish company that offers a tool developed to digitize sales management.

It support customers by dealing with three major challenges: How to track, Manage and enable performance.

The project's focus was to bring a new and fresh look to a startup that is expanding its business across the world.

Our Brief

“Going from a start-up to a scale-up, how should Goalplan position itself visually to attract the various C-Level executives in our targeted industries (retail, MLM, Retail Banking, and Telecom) across multiple continents? Being a techie Scandinavian company, how shall we appear on the international stage in order to attract both the senior decision makers and the end-users of our application?”
