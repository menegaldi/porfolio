What is Goalplan?

GoalPlan is a Swedish company that offers a tool developed to digitize sales management.

It support customers by dealing with three major challenges: How to track, Manage and enable performance.

The project's focus was to bring a new and fresh look to a startup that is expanding its business across the world.

Our Brief

“Going from a start-up to a scale-up, how should Goalplan position itself visually to attract the various C-Level executives in our targeted industries (retail, MLM, Retail Banking, and Telecom) across multiple continents? Being a techie Scandinavian company, how shall we appear on the international stage in order to attract both the senior decision makers and the end-users of our application?”

Research Phase

Together with GoalPlan, we analysed the strategic placement of the brand in relation to its competitors.

GoalPlan sees itself in the future as a modern and an expressive brand with a scandinavian touch, which is a combination lacking in the global market at the moment.

Research Insight

Using as foundation surveys and interviews, we could understand that the user really liked the services and digital products offered by GoalPlan.

Understanding Client Needs

GoalPlan had the most of the efforts focused on making the company grow internally, making sure they could offer the best service in the market, by having a digital platform user friendly, trustful and efficient. But they lacked in communication channels to sell their services to new potential clients.

As a result, all the channels didn’t transmit all the values, the identity from the company and the communcation was broken and unefficienty.

Brand Workshop

In our workshop, many branding exercises were put into practice to improve our knowledge of the company as a whole, helping them to remind why they do what they do.

Additionally, we discussed the mission, the vision, core values and several other topics to allow the creation of the visual identity later on in the process.

They want to bring innovation to the market field, having as a focus on people (from managers to end users), having as a goal to offer clarity and premium touch in their services, and yet find a good balance between being Playful and Professional

Strategy

“A support to other companies” in a nutshell.

Our Strategy Metaphorically: A Camelion Brand, where the digital platforms assume colors from client’s brands.

TYPOGRAPHY

Logo

Two of the main qualities a logo could have is to be unique and remarkable. We used a circular grid to bring a sense of friendness and playfulness, because creating a distinct shape was essencial for GoalPlan to standout from competition. To translate our strategy into a glyph, we created a bold G with steep line to give a sense of growth and success. With a single circle it is possible to rebuild the logo and how it should be represented. With this rules, it is easy to reproduce the shape of the logo without lose proportions.
